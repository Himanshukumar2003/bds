Concept of TIY (Teach it Yourself) A game changer in implementation of NEP 2020 & NCF 2023

Every child is a “Born learner”. A two-year-old cannot be taught. Yet he learns home spoken languages & more, all on his own. At age 5, we take a child out of his Natural Learning Environment, & put him into an Environment of Forced Teaching.

Developed by BDS Education Research Foundation, concept of “Teach it Yourself” (TIY) exploits this “Natural Learning Capability” of a child

Instead of teaching, by forcing a child to learn, make the content so simple, graphic, & practical based, that a child can look at it, apply his mind, and learn. The way he did it till age 5. All on his own. Teacher only checks back, provides guidance & clarifications
