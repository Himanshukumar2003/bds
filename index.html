Preview our product line

Under Skill Development, NEP 2020 has Mandated establishment of labs & teaching of coding & robotics in primary schools.

Key USP of our product line includes

  • Wide Usability Span of Products. All our products have something to teach to a students of class 1, as well as to a student of class 12/college.

  • Common Multi Language SW. These products use a three-in-one Education SW named mBlock 5. Students can code using Block based Scratch, or using inbuilt editors of Python & Arduino C.
