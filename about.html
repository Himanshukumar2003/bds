Our Vision

CODE KARGA, INDIA BADEGA

Our Mission

We believe, the best method for upliftment of any underprivileged family is to make one of its member a “Coding Professional.” We are focused on doing this

Our Aim

NEP 2020, has a similar mission. Our aim is to provide a pan India solution that removes the on ground implementation problems it is facing

Reality Check

Under Skill Development, NEP 2020 has Mandated establishment of labs & teaching of coding & robotics in primary schools.

However, Ground Reality has Implementation Problems like:

  • Cost of establishing & running a Robotic lab

  • Cost & availability of trained faculty.

  • Total non availability of faculty in small towns & rural areas

  • Problem of faculty churn due to high demand & low availability

  • Non availability of professionally created, centrally located, quality teaching material, & back-end project support

  • Inability of faculty to keep pace with technological changes

The Solution

The solution requires Mind Set Change at all levels

  • Look at “Computers & Coding” as one subject

  • Convert an existing computer lab in to a “Computer cum Coding” lab

  • Provide “Teacher Guides” with content made so simple, that existing computer faculty can first learn, & then teach

  • Make available even simpler grade wise “Student books,” to enable children to learn & practice on their own, or with a little nudge from a teacher

  • Aim at making children entrepreneurship, or job ready early in life (possibly by the time they leave school), & not run the rat races of marks.
