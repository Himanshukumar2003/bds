HaloCode AI & IoT Creator Add-on Pack comes with 13 mBuild's electronic modules and 9 accessories packs, including Speaker, Motor, LED Strip, LED Ring, LED Matrix, Fan, Ranging Sensor, PIR Sensor, Dual RGB Sensor, Angle Sensor, Extend Block, and Rechargeable Lithium Battery

Developed by Makeblock, mBuild modules are small in size but highly intelligent in functions. They are compatible with almost all mainstream open-source hardware and are easy to use. Programming is not a necessity, but more complicated functions can be achieved through programming, for instance, using mBlock 5. Currently, over 30 mBuild electronic modules have been developed, and more modules will be available. You can use mBuild modules to create projects based on your ideas, popularize programming education, promote AI education, or participate in robotic competitions.

Versatile Projects:

This set features a multitude of characteristic projects including intelligent traffic lights, a digital thermometer, an ultrasonic distance meter, climate control, a mesmerizing light show, musical instruments, a simple robot, and much more! With 65 easy and quick assembly circuits, the creative options are boundless.

Extensive Experimentation:

Dive into over 90 different experiments that engage users in hands-on learning experiences. Explore fundamental concepts of electronics and coding through practical applications and engaging projects.

High-Quality Materials:

Crafted from durable ABS material and Copper buckles, ensuring sturdiness and safety during assembly and experimentation.

Suitable for Ages 8+:

Tailored for budding innovators aged 8 and above, our Arduino Set is designed to be accessible and engaging for young learners, promoting their interest in STEM subjects from an early age.
